# Stable fluids
A C++/Linux/OpenGL implementation of a stable fluid simulator based on research by Jos Stam

## Requires
* fftw3f: http://www.fftw.org/fftw-3.3.9.tar.gz
 * configure with --enable-float
* GLFW
